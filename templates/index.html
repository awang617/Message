About Us

Acacia for secret love, daffodil for new beginnings, wisteria for welcome, and camellia for my destiny is in your hands. In Victorian times, the language of flowers was used to convey romantic expressions but over time, many of these hidden meaning have become forgotten. Some flowers are quite frank in their meanings, a red rose for love and lily for majesty, but others may be unexpected, like cactus for ardent love or peony for anger.

Inspired by the iris flower, Message is here to help you decipher the language of flowers. We have helped countless brides, couples, and pining hearts find a way to communicate through thoughtful bouquets that say more than just words alone. Let us help you find the right flower for what you want to say because every message is different.
